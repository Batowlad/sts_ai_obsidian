# Claim: hardcoded-strategy AIs won't scale to high-level play

> Author's stated opinion, not demonstrated. Status **open** pending evidence.

The author of [[2021-08-15-creating-an-ai-for-slay-the-spire]] asserts that
[[spirecomm]]'s **hardcoded strategies and priorities** approach "won't scale to
playing at a high level."

## Evidence
- **For:** author's judgment (an experienced practitioner). No benchmark given.
- **Against:** none yet. Notably, the search-based [[sts-battle-ai]] (also not learned)
  plays *very* well in combat — so "non-learned" isn't inherently weak; the claim is
  specifically about *hardcoded heuristics*, not search. Worth watching for
  counter-evidence.
